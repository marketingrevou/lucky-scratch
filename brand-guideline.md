## Logo

The logo is the key element of our visual appearance. Its clear graphic design language reduced to the essentials conveys uniqueness and boldness. Our primary logo is a circle with a 'REVOU' word knocked out of the middle, a strong ambassador of our brand.

**Spell:** "re-vo-yu"

### Logo Color

| Swatch | Hex | RGB | CMYK |
|--------|-----|-----|------|
| Yellow | `#fede3e` | R253 G222 B63 | C2 M9 Y86 K0 |
| Black | `#000000` | R0 G0 B0 | C75 M68 Y67 K90 |

### Logo — Free Space

Always maintain adequate clear space around the logo. The clear space is an integral part of its design, ensuring the logo can be seen quickly, uncluttered by other logos, symbols, artwork, or text.

- **Minimum size:** 1.5 cm

### Logo — On Yellow Background

Use drop shadow with the following ratio:
- Opacity: 20%
- Blur: 5 px
- X Offset: 0 px
- Y Offset: 0 px

### Logo — Do's and Don'ts

Inaccurate usage of the corporate signature leads to inconsistencies in communication and dilutes the brand image. It is forbidden to place the logo on a background that reduces its readability.

**Do NOT:**
1. Change the inclining position of the logo
2. Change color configuration of the logo
3. Rotate the logo
4. Change logo shape
5. Use outline as a logo
6. Take out the yellow round shape
7. Place the RevoU logo off-center from the yellow circle

## Logo — Partnership

Aligning partnership logos should follow clear space rules, with the word **"X"** (in Red Hat Regular Font) placed between the logos.

**Special case:** Remove the word "X" between logos for partnerships with more than one partner.

## Typography

### Primary Typeface — Red Hat Display

Red Hat Display is the primary typeface. It was chosen for its focus on readability, simplicity, and casual feel.

| Weight | Usage |
|--------|-------|
| **Red Hat Display Bold** | Headlines |
| Red Hat Display Regular | Body copy |

### Comic Typeface — Kalam

Kalam is the special typeface for comic content. It was chosen because it resembles an illustrator's handwriting style, giving a personal touch.

| Weight | Usage |
|--------|-------|
| **Kalam Regular** | Primary comic content |
| Kalam Light | Secondary comic content |

## Look and Feel — Corporate Branding & Full Program Ads

### Design Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Simple** | The most important thing is for the audience to understand what we are saying as efficiently as possible. What is shown and communicated must be simple. |
| **Clean** | Clean design highlights a modern and timeless mood, reflecting that RevoU's knowledge is related to modern technology and is timeless. |
| **Casual** | Casual looks balance simple and clean elements so they don't create a stiff and formal impression. RevoU's target audience is young people — communicate casually like close friends. |

### Supergraphic

The yellow round shape, besides having similar looks to the RevoU logo, carries deep meaning:
- **Round** = starting point
- **Yellow round** = moon

> At RevoU, students are assisted from the very beginning until their tech career flies high to the moon.

### Design Rules for Corporate Branding & Full Program Ads

**Corporate branding includes:** POPs needs (Corporate & Employee Branding), Corporate Website, and RevoU Community.

**Full Program Ads includes:** Full Program Ads banner & Full Stack Landing Page.

- Dominant use of neutral colors (white/grey/black) with yellow accent and vertical colors
- Use **photos** as objects (not illustrations)
- Typography on headlines uses solid style — no added effects
- RevoU logo must always be positioned at the top with flexible right/left/center placement

## Look and Feel — Organic Channel & Mini Course Ads

### Design Principles

Same principles as corporate branding, with an additional one:

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Simple** | Audience must understand the message as efficiently as possible. |
| **Clean** | Modern and timeless mood reflecting RevoU's knowledge relevance. |
| **Casual** | Communicate like close friends with young target audience. |
| **Fun** | Unique add-on theme for mini course ads and all organic channels. Mini course and organic channels use "Free" as an attraction — show the impression of a fun free class. |

### Design Looks on Social Media (Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube)

#### General Post
- Main visual objects are flexible: memes, digital imaging photos, or illustrations
- Use Red Hat font for simple & casual impression
- Typography on headlines/stickers/elements can have effects: 3D, shadow, outline stroke, etc.
- More varied colors to highlight the fun impression
- Background: neutral light gray with a touch of yellow, white, black, and pink

#### Special — Edu Vertical
- Dominant background: light gray with a touch of vertical colors + little bit of yellow
  - Magenta: Digital Marketing
  - Blue: Product Management
  - Orange: Data Analytics

#### Mini Course Poster
- Special color portion per vertical is more dominant than gray
- Helps the audience distinguish each vertical based on color
- Mini course poster design stands out in the feed

#### ESS (Expert Sharing Series) Poster
- Dominant black and yellow colors to stand out in the feed
- Uses basic round shape as a characteristic of the RevoU logo

#### Alumni Comic
- Primary colors: yellow and black, complemented by neutral colors (gray, white)
- Handwriting fonts for personal touch

#### YouTube Thumbnail ("You Ask Alumni Answer")
- Same template across all videos to give the "series" impression
- Color of each vertical + icons to distinguish topics between verticals
- Light gray background with photos of the interviewed talent

### Design Looks on Journal

Journal visual illustrations are used on headers — flexible to describe the article topic.

**Illustration style rules (non-childish):**
- Use RevoU colors: light gray, black, and yellow
- Touch of vertical colors (magenta: DM, blue: PM, orange: DA) for vertical-specific articles
- Neutral white background

**Journal topics:**
1. Profiling / One Day As
2. Edu Content
3. Students Portfolio

**Formats:**
- Header (1200 x 700)
- IG Story (1080 x 1920)
- Article Comic / Infographic

### Design Looks on Mini Course Ads

Goal: show that the class is **fun and beginner-friendly**.

**Creative categories:**
- **Regular Posters** — inform the details of the event
- **Thematic Content** — increase audience awareness of the event

**Visual rules:**
- Dominant use of vertical colors, with yellow or neutral color accents
- Typography on headlines/stickers/elements with effects: 3D, shadow, outline stroke, etc.
